I. Responsibilities

Understand your role, responsibilities, and how you contribute to the success of the team and the projects we handle, and start building your real estate journey with clarity and purpose.

Referral Agent
  • Refers potential property buyers or leads to the sales team
  • Shares basic property information to interested clients
  • Connects clients to the appropriate sales representative
  • Ensures accurate and timely submission of referral details
Property Assistant (New Agent)
  • Assists in handling client inquiries about our projects
  • Provides basic property details and requirements to clients
  • Supports documentation and coordination with buyers
  • Helps schedule property presentations or site visits
  • Updates and organizes client records and lead information
Sales Manager (Agent with Network)
  • Leads and manages a group of agents or referral partners
  • Monitors team performance and sales targets
  • Trains and supports agents in closing deals and client handling
  • Develops strategies to generate more leads and sales opportunities
Licensed Broker Partner
  • Oversees and ensures full compliance with real estate laws, regulations, and ethical standards
  • Coordinates with management for project accreditation, updates, and ongoing developer requirements
  • Serves as the official broker partner for assigned projects and developers, ensuring committed support and representation
  • Maintains strong professional alignment between the developer, company, and selling network

II. Legal Status & Liability

  • Independent Contractor: You are an independent entrepreneur, not an employee or partner.
  • Liability: You are solely responsible for any expenses or legal consequences resulting from your own negligent or unlawful acts. The Broker is not liable for your personal actions.
  • Confidentiality: Post-termination, you may not use any proprietary information or trade secrets gained during this engagement for your own benefit or that of others.

III. Termination

Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement at any time by providing notice.
